Support


We provide spare parts for our sprayers in Singapore and throughout the Asia region. By replacing worn parts, we extend the working life of our sprayers. This reduces waste and is a cheaper alternative to buying a new replacement sprayer.


Our sprayers can be customised with different accessories to fit your application requirements. These include specialized nozzles for low-volume spraying, conical hoods localised spraying and lances of various lengths.
